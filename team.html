    Anna Peca

    MD, MSc, PhD Plastic Surgeon Regenerative Medicine Specialist

    Anna Peca is a plastic surgeon and regenerative medicine specialist who founded YURON as a structured regenerative framework — not a conventional clinic.

    Drawing on training in general surgery, plastic surgery, aesthetic medicine, regenerative medicine and molecular biology, she developed YURON protocols to treat ageing inside-out — combining surgical-level understanding of tissue biology with advanced diagnostics and regenerative therapies.

    With over 15 years of clinical experience, Anna Peca holds an MSc in Regenerative Medicine and a PhD in Plastic Surgery, and has published numerous scientific articles. Her work is grounded in research and ongoing education, supporting both patients and doctors through collaboration and knowledge-sharing.

    One principle: never perform a procedure today that compromises the face in 10 years.

    Medical Education

    • Primary Medical Qualification in General Medicine (2008)
    • Bachelor of Science (BSc Hons) (2008)

    Postgraduate Clinical Training

      Completed postgraduate clinical training in:
    • General Surgery (2008–2011)
    • Plastic Surgery (2012–2014)

    Doctoral & Academic Qualifications

    • Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Plastic Surgery (2018)
    • Thesis: “The Influence of Platelet-Rich Plasma on Survival of Fat Autografts in Plastic Surgery.”
    • Master of Science (MSc) in Regenerative Medicine
    • Queen Mary University of London (2022) Thesis: “Investigation of the Role of Distinct Phosphoinositide 3-Kinase Isoforms on Migration of Keratinocytes.”
