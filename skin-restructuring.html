How this treatment works

This treatment delivers a specialised combination of hyaluronic acid and essential amino acids into the dermis — the skin layer responsible for structural support.

  • These components act as building blocks of the extracellular matrix, stimulating fibroblasts to produce collagen, elastin, and other structural proteins that maintain skin strength and elasticity.

  • Accurate injection technique also helps minimise the risk of bruising, resulting in very minimal downtime — often only a few hours.

  • The effectiveness of the treatment depends not only on what is injected, but also on precise placement within the correct skin layer. Delivering the solution at the appropriate dermal depth allows the active components to interact directly with fibroblasts and support regeneration of the skin's extracellular matrix.

  • At YURON, particular attention is given to injection depth and technique, ensuring both optimal regenerative effects and patient safety.
