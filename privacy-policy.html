YURON — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how YURON (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects and uses personal data through our website yuron.co.uk.

Scope. This policy covers personal data collected via this website only — for example through our contact form and cookies. It does not cover the clinical or medical records of our patients, which are processed separately under our clinical records system and our duty of patient confidentiality.

1. Who we are (Data Controller)

YURON MEDICINE LIMITED
18 Cadogan Gardens, London, England, SW3 2RP
Company registration number: 15517629
Contact: care@yuron.co.uk or via our contact form.

For any questions about how we use your data, please contact us using the details above.

2. What data we collect

Through this website we may collect:

  • Identification & contact data: name, email address, phone number, and anything you choose to include when using our contact form.
  • Technical / browsing data: IP address, pages visited, time and duration of visit, browser and device type.
  • Marketing consent: a record of whether you have chosen to receive our news and offers.

We do not collect medical or health information through this website. Please do not send sensitive health details via the contact form.

3. Why we use your data

  • To respond to your enquiries and contact requests.
  • To send you news and offers, where you have opted in (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • To operate, secure and improve our website.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

4. Legal basis (UK GDPR)

PurposeLegal basis
Responding to your enquiriesOur legitimate interests — responding to people who contact us (Art. 6(1)(f))
Strictly necessary cookiesOur legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f))
Marketing emails (news and offers)Your consent (Art. 6(1)(a))
Meeting legal obligationsLegal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))

Note: this website does not process special category (health) data, so no Article 9 UK GDPR condition applies to it.

5. Cookies and consent

Our website currently uses only strictly necessary cookies — technical cookies set by WordPress that are required for the website and its administration area to function. Under UK law, these cookies do not require your consent.

We do not use analytics, advertising or other non-essential cookies, and we therefore do not display a cookie consent banner.

6. International transfers

Our website is hosted within the European Economic Area (EEA). Transfers of personal data from the UK to the EEA are permitted under the UK’s adequacy regulations, so no additional transfer safeguard is required for this hosting.

If any other tool we use (for example analytics) transfers your data outside the UK and the EEA, we make sure an appropriate safeguard is in place — such as a UK adequacy decision, the ICO’s International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA), or the UK Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. Sharing your data

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Our service providers (e.g. website hosting, IT support) acting on our instructions.
  • Competent authorities where required by law.

8. How long we keep your data

DataRetention period
Contact form / enquiry data3 years from last contact
Marketing emails (news and offers)Until you unsubscribe or withdraw your consent
Technical / browsing data12 months

Clinical / patient records are retained separately, in line with UK health-records retention requirements, and are not governed by this website policy.

9. Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Have inaccurate data corrected (rectification).
  • Have your data erased (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Object to processing.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at care@yuron.co.uk or via our contact form.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO): https://ico.org.uk

10. Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, alteration or disclosure.

11. Children

This website is intended for a general adult audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children through this site.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date of the most recent update is shown at the top of this document.
