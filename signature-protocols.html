YURON Signature Protocols

YURON signature protocols are personalised medical pathways, not standard packages. Each protocol is developed around the patient's biology, concerns, goals, and long-term health.

YURON signature protocols are personalised medical pathways, not standard packages. Each protocol is developed around the patient's biology, concerns, goals, and long-term health.

They combine:
  • targeted screening
  • a personalised treatment plan
  • internal health optimisation
  • a maintenance plan
  • ongoing guidance from a multidisciplinary team

Full Face Rejuvenation Protocol

Our full face rejuvenation protocol enhances skin quality and appearance by addressing visible signs and internal causes like hormonal imbalances, nutrition, and inflammation. It combines skin treatments with overall health optimisation.

  • targeted screening
  • blood, hormonal, nutritional, gut, or genetic assessment where appropriate
  • a personalised treatment plan focused on skin quality, structural support, and volume restoration
  • internal optimisation to support collagen production and tissue response
  • a long-term maintenance plan

Hair Regeneration Protocol

Our hair regeneration protocol supports growth and scalp health by tackling hair thinning causes such as hormones, nutrition, inflammation, and genetics, combining scalp treatments with internal optimisation.

  • targeted screening
  • blood, hormonal, nutritional, gut, or genetic assessment where appropriate
  • a personalised treatment plan focused on skin quality, structural support, and volume restoration
  • internal optimisation to support collagen production and tissue response
  • a long-term maintenance plan

Pre- and Post-Surgical Protocol

Our pre- and post-surgical protocol offers personalised medical support addressing skin and internal factors affecting healing and recovery, recognizing surgery's overall stress on the body.

  • assessment of factors that may affect healing
  • optimisation of nutritional and biochemical status
  • tissue preparation before surgery
  • personalised recovery support after surgery
  • regenerative support where appropriate
  • follow-up by the relevant specialists

True Age Reset Protocol

Our longevity protocol starts with optimising basics like deficiencies, hormones, and gut health before advanced therapies, ensuring a strong foundation and involving specialists when needed.

  • in-depth screening
  • optimisation of nutritional and biochemical status
  • correction of key deficiencies and imbalances
  • hormonal, gut, metabolic, and inflammatory optimisation
  • specialist referral where needed
  • advanced longevity therapies only when appropriate
  • long-term monitoring and maintenance
